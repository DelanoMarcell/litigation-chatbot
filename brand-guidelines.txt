Nexa Corporate Brand Identity Guide
1. Brand Overview
Nexa is a legal technology company delivering beautifully engineered, African-forward, and human-centric digital solutions for the legal industry. The brand reflects a blend of innovation, elegance, and empowerment. Our visual identity is a key part of our story—communicating clarity, intelligence, and design integrity.
2. Logo Guidelines
Primary Logo: The main Nexa logo consists of the stylized wordmark with a single missing stroke on the 'N' and a burnt rose accent dot. This should be used on all digital platforms, marketing materials, and signage. Maintain adequate spacing around the logo.
Tagline: “Beyond Law. Powered by Nexa.”
When to Use:
- Use the tagline on homepages, pitch decks, banners, and digital ads.
- Avoid tagline usage in compact formats like app icons, footers, or favicons.
3. Color Palette
Primary Color – Plum: #3F1D3A
Accent Color – Burnt Rose: #D67D6E
Neutral Light – Blush Beige: #F8ECEA
Contrast Dark – Charcoal: #1E1E1E
4. Typography
Primary Typeface: Sora
Use Sora for all web and print content. Employ bold weights for headings, regular for body, and avoid decorative fonts to ensure legibility.
5. Brand Voice and Messaging
Nexa’s voice is professional, empowering, and modern. We speak with clarity, and purpose. Content should be written in plain English, with an emphasis on practical outcomes, user-centric design, and African innovation.
6. Application Examples
- Website header and hero sections
- Business cards and email signatures
- LegalTech product interfaces
- Thought leadership and event materials
- Social media banners and posts
